What is resilience?

Resilience is what gives people the strength to cope with hardship and stress. It is the psychological strength that helps us cope with tough times and recover quickly from difficulties. During this global pandemic it is of monumental importance that we be resilient and win this war against the pandemic. Resilient people are able to handle adversities better than many others. Lockdown has significantly lowered people's health metally; therefore, it is important that we stay resilient and strong.

Dealing with change or loss is inevitable in life; however, it depends on how we deal and handle problems. Many deal with endure exreme stress, but now everyone is dealing with this pandemic and are experienceing stress. I must repeat and emphasize the fact that you must be resilient during these times. Have you ever wondered why some people stay calm during disasters and others do not? This is what separates resilient people from non-resilient people.
